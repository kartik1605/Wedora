Our Story

Born From a Passion
For Perfection

Wedora was born in the heart of Udaipur — the City of Lakes — with a singular vision: to create wedding experiences so extraordinary, they become the stories couples tell for generations.

What began as a small décor studio has grown into Udaipur's most trusted full-service wedding experience company. We've had the honour of crafting over 200 events across Rajasthan's most iconic venues — from palace hotels on the shores of Lake Pichola to the majestic ramparts of Kumbhalgarh Fort.

We believe every wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime masterpiece. That belief drives everything we do — from the first consultation to the final petal, from the grandest stage to the smallest detail that only the couple will notice.

At Wedora, we don't just plan weddings. We compose royal experiences.
