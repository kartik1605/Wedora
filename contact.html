Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book Wedora?

We recommend reaching out at least 6–12 months in advance for peak wedding season (October–March). However, we can accommodate shorter timelines — reach out and we'll do our best to make it work.

Do you handle destination weddings outside Udaipur?

Absolutely. We travel anywhere in Rajasthan — Kumbhalgarh, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer, Chittorgarh, and beyond. We also take on destination events across India for the right clients.

Can you work within a specific budget?

Yes. We've worked across a wide range of budgets and always prioritise maximum impact within your investment. During our first consultation, we'll understand your priorities and craft a proposal accordingly.

Do you offer single services or only full packages?

Both. We can manage your complete wedding experience from start to finish, or handle specific elements like just the décor, just the stage setup, or just vendor coordination — whatever you need.

How does the booking process work?

It starts with a consultation call or WhatsApp chat. We understand your vision, propose a concept and quote, refine it together, and then secure your date with a booking amount. Simple and transparent.

Will I have a dedicated coordinator on the wedding day?

Yes, always. Every Wedora wedding has a dedicated on-ground coordinator and team present throughout the event to ensure everything runs flawlessly so you can be fully present in the moment.
