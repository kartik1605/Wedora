At Wedora Decor Planners, we create immersive wedding experiences inspired by emotion, culture, and timeless aesthetics.

From intimate celebrations by the lakes of Udaipur to grand destination weddings across Rajasthan, every detail is thoughtfully designed to feel personal, elegant, and unforgettable.

More than decor, we craft atmospheres — where florals, lighting, textures, hospitality, and storytelling come together seamlessly.

Rooted in tradition and refined through modern design, every Wedora celebration is created with intention.
